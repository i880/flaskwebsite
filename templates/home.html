Welcome to the Pose Estimation App!

This app allows you to track your exercise movements using a webcam. Follow the steps below to get started:

  1. Ensure your webcam is properly connected and accessible.
  2. Set the desired number of series and repetitions for your exercise.
  3. Click "Start Exercise" to begin tracking your movements in real-time.
  4. To stop the exercise, click the "Stop Exercise" button.
  5. You can reset your count at any time by clicking the "Reset" button or press 'r' on your keyboard.
